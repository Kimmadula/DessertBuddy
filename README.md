> **Your AI-Powered Sweet Companion**

DessertBuddy is a personal AI assistant designed for all things sweet. Whether you need recipes, baking tips, or dessert recommendations, DessertBuddy is here to help!

## Features

- **AI Chatbot**: Powered by Flowise, get instant answers to your baking questions.
- **Featured Desserts**: Browse a curated selection of sweet treats including:
  - Chocolate Cake
  - Chocolate Chip Cookies
  - Vanilla Cupcakes
  - Cheesecake
  - Apple Pie
  - Vanilla Ice Cream

## Technology Stack

- **HTML5**
- **CSS3**
- **JavaScript (ES Modules)**
- **Flowise Embed** (Chatbot Integration)

## Usage

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser.
2. Interact with the chatbot in the bottom right corner (or embedded area) for assistance.
3. Scroll through the featured dessert cards for inspiration.
